Prices are in Ethiopian Birr and include VAT where applicable. Prices are subject to change without notice.

Delivery is available within Addis Ababa. Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Same-day delivery available for orders placed before 2:00 PM.

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After 1 hour, a 50% cancellation fee applies.
